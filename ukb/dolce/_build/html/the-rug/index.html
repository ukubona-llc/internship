2.1 Measuring Risk in the Body

  • Perioperative mortality, ESRD, long-term mortality
  • Matched donor vs. nondonor comparisons
  • Custom phenotype projections (e.g. 40F, 84M)
  • Wide confidence intervals = visible epistemic humility

2.2 Hospitalization as a Sentinel

  • 40+ year dataset: 70% hospitalized post-nephrectomy
  • Older donors + post-op diabetes/hypertension = higher risk
  • Frailty conspicuously absent from registries
  • Hospitalization = the canary in the epistemic coal mine
