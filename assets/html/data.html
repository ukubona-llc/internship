Data Sources

Data was collected using pybaseball, an open-source Python wrapper for Baseball Savant’s Statcast data. We focused on the following time periods and performance metrics for Juan Soto:

  • Late 2024 (Yankees): August to September 2024, when Soto batted in front of Aaron Judge.
  • Early 2025 (Mets): March to May 2025, after Soto signed with the New York Mets and no longer had Judge in the lineup.

Key metrics extracted:

  • Hits per game
  • Rolling average (10-game window)
  • Plate appearances, walk rate, and pitch zone %
