Symbolic Logic
Discipline: Logic, Philosophy
Applications: Mathematics, Computer Science

Introduction

Symbolic logic is the study of abstract symbols and formal systems used to represent logical expressions and reasoning processes.

Background

In the Ukubona framework, symbolic logic bridges intuitive perception with formalized epistemology, clarifying how humans structure knowledge.
